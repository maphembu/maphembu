Who is Phembu?

I exist to Encourage, Empower and Transform.

Phembumuzi is a name with a rich legacy that continues to inspire! The dynamic duo who brought him into this world—his Divine, mother and father envisioned it as a beacon for someone bold enough to invest in his family and elevate them into new heights. Now, He proudly carry the name Phembumuzi, Ma'Phembu; rekindle and taking the legacy even further. Throughout his life, He’ve approached every challenge with enthusiasm, creativity, and a ceaseless desire to achieve success. This passion and drive is paving the way to countless opportunities, unique experiences and exceptional relationships both personally and professionally.

Professional Background. With experience in both government and retail sectors, he transitioned into the tech industry in 2022, joining Sage as an intern. Since then, he has played a key role in the Renewals team, contributing to contract management and customer retention efforts. His dedication and performance led to securing a contract extension for the Payroll ALF Project, marking a significant professional milestone. Building on this success, he expanded his expertise into Order Operations as a Sales Administrator, where he continues to drive efficiency and process improvements. With a strategic mindset and a results-oriented approach, he remains committed to delivering value and fostering growth within his team.
